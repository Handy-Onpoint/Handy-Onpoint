Who We Are

We are fresh on the scene and eager to gain customer appreciation. We started small and now built up through word of mouth and customer referrals. It’s simply a tribute to the treatment our clients received with every visit, and every repair.

Handy Onpoint was established in 2021. Through diligence we’ve grown our customer base and look to gain more business. This thing started with the owner, who worked under his father, and grandfather as a kid. Those experiences inspired him to complete trades programs, and take it even another notch to build a Chicagoland area Handyman Service. His passion for leaving every customer with a smile has led to a warm hometown reputation. Our clients love us.
